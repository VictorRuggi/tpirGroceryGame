PRICE IS RIGHT - GROCERY GAME

By purchasing grocery items that are only part of the 5 items below, reach a total that is between $20 and $22 to win the game. You can even buy one item in multiple amounts (for example, 3 boxes of waffles) to get closer to $20-$22, but be careful: if you go over $20 and $22 at any point in the game, you lose. On that same subject, if you use up all 5 items in your purchase and fall short of $20-$22, you lose. You must have a total that is between $20 and $22 within 5 guesses to win the game.
